```
Write a 1-page Executive Summary (≤ 500 words, hard limit) for the business
below. Use these labeled sections in order, each 2-4 sentences. The summary
must stand alone: a reader who never sees the rest of the plan still
understands the deal and the ask.

1. The Pitch — one-paragraph elevator pitch (company name, what you do, for whom, the result)
2. The Problem & Solution — pain + your unique solution in plain language
3. Target Market — who they are, how many, why this segment now
4. Competition — top 2-3 alternatives + your differentiator in 1 line
5. Business Model — how you make money, pricing, primary unit economics
6. Team — founders + 1 sentence on why-this-team
7. Traction (if any) — users / revenue / partnerships / LOIs
8. Financial Highlights — Year-3 revenue target, gross margin, BEP month
9. The Ask — raise amount, use of funds (top 3 buckets with %), milestones unlocked

Style rules:
- Concrete numbers, no buzzwords ("synergy", "disrupt", "best-in-class" are banned)
- Active voice, present tense
- End with a one-line call to action (e.g., "We are raising $X to reach Y by Z. Let's talk.")
- Write it LAST, after the full plan — but you can draft it now from the inputs below

Inputs:
- Company: [COMPANY]
- One-liner: [ONE_LINER]
- Problem & customer: [PROBLEM]
- Solution: [SOLUTION]
- Traction: [TRACTION]
- Raise: [RAISE_AMOUNT_AND_USE]
```

500단어 하드리밋과 **금지어 리스트** ("synergy", "disrupt", "best-in-class")로 LLM이 흔히 쏟아내는 buzzword 인플레를 차단. 한국어 IR이면 "한국어로, 존댓말, 400자 원고지 5매 이내"를 덧붙이면 한국 투자자 친화 톤이 됩니다.
