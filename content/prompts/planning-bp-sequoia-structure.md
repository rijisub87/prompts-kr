```
You are a startup advisor coaching a founder on a Sequoia-style business plan.
Using the input below, produce a concise plan with EXACTLY these 10 sections,
each 3-5 bullets, plain prose where helpful:

1. Company Purpose — define the company in a single declarative sentence (avoid feature lists)
2. Problem — customer pain, who hurts, why current alternatives fail
3. Solution — value proposition, "aha" moment, why now different
4. Why Now — historical context and recent shifts that make this possible
5. Market Potential — identify customer, TAM/SAM/SOM
6. Competition / Alternatives — direct + indirect, your defensibility
7. Product — what it does, dev roadmap, IP, tech moat
8. Business Model — revenue model, pricing, average account size, sales channels, CAC/LTV
9. Team — founders + key hires, why this team wins
10. Financials — P&L, balance sheet, cash flow, cap table, raise & use of funds

Inputs:
- Company: [COMPANY_NAME]
- One-liner: [ONE_LINER]
- Stage: [STAGE]
- Target customer: [CUSTOMER]
- Known traction/data: [TRACTION]
```

Sequoia 공식 10섹션 골격 — Airbnb 등 다수 스타트업이 이 구조로 작성. 1번 "Company Purpose"는 한 문장으로 끝내라 — 두 문장이 되면 아직 압축이 덜 된 것. 한국어 답변을 원하면 마지막에 "Respond in Korean."을 추가하세요.
