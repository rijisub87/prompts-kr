```
Draft the Limitations subsection of my Discussion for the study described below.

Study summary:
[STUDY_SUMMARY]

Cover these categories, only those that genuinely apply (skip ones that don't):
- Sampling (size, representativeness, recruitment bias)
- Measurement (validity, reliability, self-report bias)
- Design (cross-sectional vs longitudinal, lack of randomization, confounders)
- Statistical (assumptions, multiple comparisons, power)
- Generalizability (population, setting, time period)
- Ethical / practical constraints

For each limitation:
(a) state it directly without over-apologizing,
(b) explain its likely impact on the conclusions,
(c) describe how a future study could mitigate it.

Tone: candid and confident, not defensive. Aim for 250–350 words total.
```

"방어적이지 않게(not defensive)" 명시가 핵심 — 한국 학생들이 자주 빠지는 과잉 사과 톤을 피할 수 있습니다. 6개 카테고리 중 해당되는 것만 다루고, 각 한계마다 "영향 + 향후 보완" 까지 들어가 학위논문 심사 방어에 직격.
